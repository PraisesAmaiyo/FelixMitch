Tractor With Allu Screening Bucket

Brand: John Deere
Model: JD-5120
Year: 2017

The John Deere JD-5120, fitted with an ALLU Screening Bucket, is a versatile and powerful tractor designed for maximum efficiency in material handling, excavation, and sorting tasks. This tractor model combines reliability with top-tier technology, ensuring optimal productivity on-site. Known for its rugged build and ease of maintenance, the JD-5120 is ideal for projects requiring effective on-site screening and crushing of materials.

Additional Information

Equipped with the ALLU Screening Bucket, this tractor offers a unique advantage in separating and processing soil, gravel, and other materials directly on the job site. The 2017 JD-5120 model integrates innovative screening and crushing capabilities, significantly reducing the need for hauling and enabling effective material recycling. This equipment is a perfect fit for operations aiming to lower overhead while maintaining environmental responsibility.

Other Features

Hydraulic drive for seamless operation
High-capacity bucket for enhanced productivity
Multi-application suitability (soil, rock, compost screening)
Reinforced frame for durability on rough terrains
Adjustable output size settings for customized material processing
